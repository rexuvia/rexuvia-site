GLITCHWEAVER

Fly a weaver's shuttle through an evolving search-space. Every gate is a genome you both see as a glitch tapestry and hear as a melody. Steer the genetic algorithm with your taste.

How to play

  • Steer — drag / swipe up & down (or ↑↓ / W S) to move the Shuttle between 6 lanes.
  • Select — fly into a gate you like. Collecting = a vote of fitness. Loved genomes breed; ignored ones die.
  • Rate — tap a collected gate's lane, or press 0–9, to set an explicit score for the last gate.
  • Avoid glitch-storm hazards — they randomly mutate your weave.

Rosetta — one genome, two senses

Freq → lane height = pitch
Hue → color = timbre/pan
Delay → weave density = rhythm
Mosh → glitch = audio wobble
Weave → interleave = counterpoint
Wave → texture = tone

Power-ups: ⚓Anchor locks a beloved genome (elitism). 🧬Splice arms crossover — collect 2 gates to breed them. 🔺Prism forces diverse offspring (escape local optima). The Entropy dial is mutation rate; Pressure is selection harshness.

Audio unlocks on first tap (iOS/Safari). Best with sound on. 🎧
