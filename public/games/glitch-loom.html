Quick Loom Guide
How to weave a glitch
  • Load art with Upload or spin up a procedural base via ✦ Generate.
  • Tune Weave for structure, Sort for warp glitching, and Mosh for chaotic bleed.
  • Tap ✦ Weave Glitch anytime — you can iterate as you tweak controls.
  • Need motion? Hop into Anim, press ▶ Preview, then export your GIF.
Tip On touch devices, sliders respond to swipes anywhere on the track — use the cyan live readout to precise dial-ins.
